About Just Fall LOL

Description

Just Fall LOL is an online multiplayer arcade game in which you play as a penguin! After the game begins, there are eight players from around the world.
In each game, there are eight real players, including you, who control cute penguins. You'll have to compete with them! The goal is to stay on the ice as long as possible and to be the last person to fall into the water. There are a lot of levels, interesting puzzles, and well-deserved victories in store for you!

Just Fall LOL- a cute little penguin awaits your arrival, to whom you can give a name and a color preference. You can request a specific game from certain participants or invite someone to your game if you wish. You can practice managing your penguin in the Practice section.

How to play

The A, S, D, and W buttons on the keyboard allow the penguin to move forward, backward, left, and right.
"Space" - jump.
The right mouse button allows you to dive.
Button "Tab" - running, acceleration.
